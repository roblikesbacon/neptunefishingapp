Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account and my data?

Open Neptune, go to Settings → Delete Account, and confirm. This permanently deletes your account and every catch and trip you've logged — it can't be undone. If you're having trouble accessing the app to do this yourself, email us and we'll take care of it.

I logged a catch but it's not showing up on my other device

Neptune logs everything offline-first, then syncs automatically once you have a signal. If something looks stuck, open Settings → Sync Status and tap Retry All. If it's still stuck after that, email us with your account email and roughly when the catch was logged.

What states does Neptune cover?

Neptune currently supports New York, New Jersey, and Pennsylvania, with stocking data and real-time conditions for tri-state waters. More states are on the way.

How do I switch between Imperial and Metric units?

Settings → Units. The change applies immediately across the whole app, including past entries.

Is my data private?

Yes. Neptune has no ads, no social feed, and doesn't sell your data. Your catch history is yours — it's used only to power your own personal bite-pattern insights. See our Privacy Policy for details.
